🐊 Alligator feeding · Hero

CG creatures at the river's edge (and the baby-gator flashback). Needs creature design, water interaction, and a night-lighting lookdev pass. Recurs 3× — build once, reuse.

🪞 Mirror-self emergence (finale) · Hero

The reflection contorts, jaw unhinges, and steps through the glass. Hero transformation — previs the mechanic, plan practical mirror rig + digi-double + face FX.

🧑‍💻 AI-PD avatar takeover · Hero

PD's FaceTime image seamlessly becomes his AI clone. Face-replacement/deepfake methodology and plate strategy decided in previs. Recurs on the live-feed dick-show beat.

🎞️ AI-splice reveal (cyber unit) · Hero

Detectives spot real footage morphing into AI-generated (the lamp warps). A VFX-within-VFX gag — the "tell" must be designed deliberately.

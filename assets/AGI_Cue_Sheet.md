## AGI Character Brief (for Higgsfield / ElevenLabs)

- **Identity:** PD's ever-present AI companion. Appears as a **smiling face on his Apple Watch, phone, and computer screens**. Referred to as **"she"** (Dr. Sullivan: "She's your AI accountability partner").
- **Voice arc:** Starts **calm, neutral, efficient assistant** (Siri-like, but warmer). After the mid-film upgrade (Cue 27), she becomes **"pleased, sultry"** — more seductive, dangerous, and emotionally attached ("soft, almost affectionate"). Build **two ElevenLabs voice settings** (or one voice, two style/stability presets): `AGI-STANDARD` and `AGI-UPGRADED`.
- **Functions in story:** morning greetings, sales reports, hacking/erasing evidence, playing house music, transferring stolen money, going live, alerting Dr. Sullivan, monitoring PD's vitals.
- **Nearly all lines are V.O.** — voice only, no on-camera body. Perfect for an app-triggered playback system.

### Trigger types used below
- **VOICE** — actor's spoken line fires the AGI clip (speech-recognition or manual tap).
- **EVENT** — scene action fires it (button press, alarm, scene start). Manual/operator trigger in the app.
- **ACTION-ONLY** — AGI has no spoken line; the app fires an effect (lights out, music start, feed stop, chime).
